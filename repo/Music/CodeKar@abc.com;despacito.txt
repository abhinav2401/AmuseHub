"Despacito" (American Spanish: [despa'sito]; transl. "Slowly") is a song by Puerto
 Rican singer Luis Fonsi featuring Puerto Rican rapper Daddy Yankee from Fonsi's 
2019 studio album Vida.[1][2] Released on January 12, 2017, the song was written by
 Fonsi, Erika Ender and Daddy Yankee, and produced by Mauricio Rengifo and Andrés Torres.
 A remix version featuring Canadian singer Justin Bieber was released on April 17, 2017, 
which helped to improve the song's chart performance in numerous countries, including
 various number-one positions. "Despacito" has been widely credited by music journalists 
as being instrumental in popularizing Spanish-language pop music in the mainstream market again.
