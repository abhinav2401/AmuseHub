"Memories" is a song by American pop band Maroon 5, released through 222 and Interscope Records on September 20, 2019.[1][2]

Lyrically, the song pays homage to the memories of a loved one who has since passed. The song is a simplified version of "Canon 
in D Major" by German composer Johann Pachelbel. It was produced by lead vocalist Adam Levine and production team the Monsters 
and the Strangerz, and written by Levine, Stefan Johnson, Jordan Johnson, Michael Pollack, Jacob Kasher Hindlin, Jon Bellion, 
and Vincent Ford. The single peaked at number two on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart.
